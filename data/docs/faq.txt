Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the notice period?
A: The notice period after confirmation is 30 days.

Q: How many annual leave days are available?
A: Employees receive 18 days of annual leave per year.

Q: Can employees work remotely?
A: Yes, employees can work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Q: How should leave be requested?
A: Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.

Q: What is the probation period?
A: The probation period is 6 months.
