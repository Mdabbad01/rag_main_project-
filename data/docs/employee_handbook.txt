Employee Handbook

Welcome to ABC Tech Solutions. This handbook provides important information for all employees.

Working Hours:
The standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Code of Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow ethical standards.

Remote Work:
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Probation Period:
All new employees are on a 6-month probation period.

Notice Period:
The standard notice period after confirmation is 30 days.
